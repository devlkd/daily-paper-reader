## 摘要
R-FLEX是一种紧凑、低零件数、基于柔性机构的径向定位机构，专为下一代大规模并行光纤馈送光谱望远镜仪器而设计。当前的仪器如暗能量光谱仪（DESI）在10.4毫米间距上使用了5000个光纤定位机器人，而未来的巡天需要2.5至3倍更高的封装密度，因此需要新的定位技术。R-FLEX机构将柔性基座处的小角度旋转转换为光纤尖端处的大行程、几乎无倾斜的径向运动，在紧凑的5.8毫米直径封装内实现了天然低背隙的线性运动。与旋转θ台耦合后，可在6.2毫米间距上实现重叠的圆形巡天区域，正如Spec-S5等项目所设想。开发过程涵盖参数化建模与优化、有限元分析、制造和原型测试。通过光学定心表征的原型单元在3.9毫米行程范围内，经校正的径向精度始终优于4微米RMS，最大光纤倾斜为0.092度，在所需行程范围内离焦42微米，并且在工作和生存极端温度下，每个机器人可承受超过40万次目标定位，具有优异的耐久性。初步的Spec-S5对完整R-θ机器人的精度、离焦和光纤倾斜要求已作规定，而这些径向台的结果超越了这些要求，为配套的θ台留出了余量。一套参数化优化流程使R-FLEX成为一款通用、可大规模生产的平台，能够针对Spec-S5以外的精密定位应用重新优化。这为收集数亿条光谱以开展宇宙大尺度结构新研究提供了所需的精度、紧凑性和可靠性。

## Abstract
R-FLEX is a compact, low-part-count flexure-based radial positioning mechanism designed for the next generation of massively parallel fiber-fed spectroscopic telescope instruments. Current instruments such as the Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument (DESI) employ 5,000 robotic fiber positioners at 10.4 mm pitch, whereas future surveys require 2.5-3x higher packing density, necessitating new positioning technologies. The R-FLEX mechanism converts small rotations at the flexure base into large, nearly tilt-free radial motion at the fiber tip, achieving naturally low-backlash linear motion within a compact 5.8 mm diameter package. Coupled with a rotating theta-stage, this enables overlapping circular patrol areas at 6.2 mm pitch, as envisioned for projects like Spec-S5. Development proceeded through parametric modeling and optimization, finite element analysis, fabrication, and prototype testing. Prototype units characterized by optical centroiding achieved a corrected radial accuracy consistently better than 4 um RMS over a 3.9 mm travel range, with maximum fiber tilt 0.092 deg, defocus 42 um over the required range, and durability over more than 400,000 targets per robot across operating and survival temperature extremes. Preliminary Spec-S5 accuracy, defocus, and fiber tilt requirements are specified for the complete R-theta robot, and these radial-stage results exceed those requirements, leaving margin for the companion theta-stage. A parametric optimization pipeline makes R-FLEX a versatile, mass-producible platform that can be re-optimized for precision-positioning applications beyond Spec-S5. This offers the precision, compactness, and reliability needed to collect hundreds of millions of spectra for new studies of the large-scale structure of the universe.
