## 摘要
了解变形、机械柔性相控阵的形状对于保持其波束方向图的能力起着重要作用。本文介绍了集成基于曲率的形状传感的此类阵列的设计、建模和实验验证。我们在一组具有代表性的变形形状上分析了在测量噪声下有效变形传感所需的传感器密度；开发并实验验证了将测量应变与局部电路板曲率相关联的物理模型；并展示了在贴合和自由悬挂场景下，一个具有形状感知能力的6 GHz八单元阵列的端到端设计，在曲率半径低于3.6 cm时，平均重建精度达到约6%，同时恢复了波束转向性能。该系统利用了一个低成本、八通道、360°数字控制移相器平台，适用于广泛的智能天线实验。形状从低速表面测量中恢复，独立于射频条件且无需反馈，使得该方法成为一种通用传感模态，其应用范围超出了本文所展示的波束校正。

## Abstract
Knowledge of the shape of deformed, mechanically flexible phased arrays plays an important role in the ability to maintain their beam pattern. This work presents the design, modeling, and experimental validation of such arrays with integrated curvature-based shape sensing. We analyze, over a large set of representative deformed shapes, the sensor density required for effective deformation sensing under measurement noise; develop and experimentally validate a physical model relating measured strain to local board curvature; and demonstrate an end-to-end design of a shape-aware, 6-GHz, eight-element array in conformed and free-hanging scenarios, achieving an average reconstruction accuracy of ~6% while recovering beam-steering performance at radii of curvature below 3.6 cm. The system utilizes a low-cost, eight-channel, 360$^{\circ}$ digitally controlled phase-shifter platform useful for a wide range of smart-antenna experiments. The shape is recovered from low-rate surface measurements, independently of RF conditions and without feedback, making the approach a general sensing modality applicable beyond the beam correction demonstrated here.
