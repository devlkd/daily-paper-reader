## 摘要
我们展示了一种3D打印的毫米波倍频程嵌入式波导-共面波导过渡结构，旨在实现具有密集片上光谱仪阵列的焦平面。这些阵列将通过毫米波强度映射对宇宙大尺度结构进行引人入胜的巡天观测。该过渡结构由一个四级脊波导变压器组成，通过铟凸点键合形成的电气连接，将光从矩形波导耦合到共面波导。我们开发了一种容忍度感知优化方法，以识别出对制造变化具有鲁棒性的高性能过渡几何形状；该公式可应用于其他对公差敏感的设计问题。我们还描述了用于将硅芯片与金属化3D打印组件进行凸点键合的自定义设备和程序的实现。我们详细介绍了共面波导芯片和三维波导结构的制造、测试器件的仿真与计量，以及该器件的室温反射率测量。室温计量和反射测量结果与一个模型一致，该模型预测在85-170 GHz频率范围内的低温条件下耦合效率约为95%。

## Abstract
We present a 3D-printed millimeter-wave, octave-bandwidth, in-line waveguide-to-coplanar-waveguide transition designed to enable focal planes with dense arrays of on-chip spectrometers. These arrays will enable compelling surveys of the large-scale structure of the universe through millimeter-wave intensity mapping. The transition consists of a four-step ridge-waveguide transformer that couples light from a rectangular waveguide onto a coplanar waveguide via an electrical connection made with indium bump bonds. We develop a tolerance-aware optimization approach to identify high-performance transition geometries that are robust to manufacturing variations; the same formulation can be applied to other tolerance-sensitive design problems. We also describe the implementation of a custom apparatus and procedure for bump-bonding a silicon chip to a metallized 3D-printed component. We detail the fabrication of the coplanar waveguide chip and three-dimensional waveguide structure, simulations and metrology of a test device, and room temperature reflectance measurements of this device. The room temperature metrology and reflection measurements are consistent with a model that predicts a coupling efficiency of $\mathord{\sim} 95\%$ at cryogenic temperatures in the 85-170 GHz frequency range.
